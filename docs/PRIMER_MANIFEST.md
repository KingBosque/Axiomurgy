# Primer manifest

These files are local copies of the seven uploaded primer transcripts that seeded Axiomurgy.
They are included here so the repo remains self-contained when handed to another agent or IDE.

- `primers/primer_01.txt` — power-system taxonomy, including wild / definitive / thematic / pluralistic / synergistic / meta
- `primers/primer_02.txt` — magical culture, messy belief, actual rules versus believed rules
- `primers/primer_03.txt` — four common forms of magic: spells, items, divine intervention, enhancements
- `primers/primer_04.txt` — critique of systematized magic and the loss of mystery
- `primers/primer_05.txt` — making magic feel like science and allowing a competitive meta to evolve
- `primers/primer_06.txt` — Witch Hat Atelier as programming-like rune magic with problem solving over power scaling
- `primers/primer_07.txt` — worldbuilding effects of magic, from class and war to daily life and religion
